Who am I?

I'm Bernardo, a Portuguese Software Developer.

From a really young age, ever since I played my first videogame Sonic the Hedgehog (1991), I got interested in the medium and its potential to create life changing interactive experiences.

I have always been interested in their design and aesthetics, but I always wanted to know HOW people created in the digital world, and make a videogame myself.

So becoming a Programmer, was something I set my sights on.
Eventually, when I finally created my first program and I saw the computer doing what I wrote in a script...

I got hooked. The rest is history.
